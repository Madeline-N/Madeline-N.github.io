The Ideal Travel Location: PEI

As someone who has been to the spectacular province of PEI I can say in full
confidence it's one of the most ideal locations for your summer vacation.
PEI has plenty of wonderful things, lovely beaches, beautiful towns and tourist
sites, wildlife and nature, and fresh tasty seafood.

The Beaches:

PEI has many beautiful beaches. The beaches in PEI are known for their red
hue, this is due to the iron-oxidize content in the soil, which gives the beach
a unique look and looks stunning in photos! Unlike other beach locations around
the world you can find many quiet beaches and hidden gems around your area!

Here are some of the most popular beaches in PEI:

  1. Cavendish Beach
  2. Brackley Beach
  3. Thunder Cove Beach
  4. North Rustico Beach
  5. Cabot Beach Provincial Park
Whilst Cavendish Beach (A tourist favourite) is quite a while from Charlotte Town
you can always find a wonderful beach on the island of PEI

Towns and Tourism:

Most populated cities/towns in PEI (2021)

  1. Charlottetown (38,809)
  2. Summerside (16,001)
  3. Stratford (10,927)
Due to the small population size PEI can give you a relaxing vacation experience
without any of the downsides of a small populated area.

Consider these experieces for your trip:

  • Boating and Sailing
  • Biking
  • Hiking and Walking trails
  • Visit the Green Gables Heritage Place
  • Visiting museums and art galleries

Wildlife and Nature


PEI is known for having wonderful natural sights especially in their Natural Park.
We've already covered the beaches but PEI has many wonderful forests, fields, and rivers.
Yu can explore these sites through trails, tours, boats, and bike paths, which makes PEI
a great place for those who are adventurous and outdoorsy.

PEI is also home to many wonderful creatures. Admire the wildlife, go fishing or
bird-watching on your trip!
Animals in PEI include: Whales, foxes, coyotes, ducks, hummingbirds, salmon, trout, ect.

Food

Now the best part, the food. PEI is known for its seafood! Every resturant in the province is guaranteed fresh seafood!
Here's some popular seafood!:

  • Lobster

  • Lobster Rolls
